Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 16, 2025

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This application is designed to function without collecting or sharing any of your personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not, and will never, collect, store, or transmit any personal data from you. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, location, or any other identifiable information. The application operates entirely on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Share

Since we do not collect any of your data, we have nothing to share with any third party. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to anyone.

3. Application Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the application requests the following permissions. These permissions are used only for the stated purpose and are not used to collect any of your data.

  • Bluetooth Access: This permission is required solely to allow the application to connect to and communicate with your nearby Bluetooth devices. No data from this connection is recorded or stored by us.
  • Device Storage Access: This permission is required to allow you to load your own files (such as documents, images, or other media) from your device's storage into the application. The application only accesses files that you specifically choose to load. We do not scan your storage or collect any files.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever update this policy, we will post the changes on this page. Our core promise of not collecting or sharing your personal data will not change.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at Dokusho.Support@proton.me.
